Pricing & Service Tiers

HOAMX is designed to be adopted in stages. Bronze is the minimum required service level, Silver mirrors traditional full-service management, and Gold adds advanced communications and AI intelligence as your community’s needs grow.

Bronze

Minimum required services. For boards that want clear records, compliant notices, billing, and simple communication tracking while keeping most tasks in-house.

Silver

Traditional full-service management. For associations that want HOAMX plus day-to-day operational support handling calls, billing, maintenance coordination, and standard reporting.

Gold

Advanced communications and AI intelligence. For communities that want a full digital office: software, operations, and enhanced communication, summaries, and analytics layered on top.

Three ways to run your HOA with HOAMX

Each tier builds on the previous one. Bronze is the minimum platform and record-keeping layer. Silver adds traditional operational support. Gold layers in advanced communications and AI-assisted workflows.

Bronze

Focus: Core Records, Billing & Compliance

Best for: Smaller or self-managed boards that want the minimum required services: clear records, reliable billing, and clean documentation without fully outsourcing management.

  • Telegram communications archive (mail, email, voicemail, SMS)
  • Document Vault for bylaws, budgets, and insurance
  • Board Governance basics (motions, meetings, minutes)
  • Core Directory for residents, board, and vendors
  • Billing & Payments (dues, assessments, late fees)
  • Standard Reports & Audits (annual summary exports)

Outcome: A clean, searchable history of decisions, notices, and financials that supports board continuity and Minnesota CIC record-keeping expectations — the minimum HOAMX service level for any association.

Silver

Focus: Traditional Full-Service Management

Best for: Boards that want HOAMX plus a traditional management experience: someone to answer phones, process payments, triage maintenance, and prepare routine reports.

  • Everything in Bronze, plus:
  • Maintenance & Work Orders (resident → manager → vendor)
  • Enhanced Directory & Contact Center with call routing & voicemail
  • Monthly financial reporting and reconciled ledgers
  • Standard vendor coordination (landscape, snow, basic maintenance)
  • More detailed reports (payments, delinquencies, maintenance spend)

Outcome: Fewer lost messages, clearer workflows, and a single system where residents, vendors, and the board interact without the board carrying all the day-to-day workload.

Gold

Focus: Advanced Communications & AI Intelligence

Best for: Associations that want the HOAMX platform, full operational support, and enhanced communication and insight tools: summaries, analytics, and AI-assisted drafting and tracking.

  • Everything in Silver, plus:
  • Advanced Telegram tools (AI summaries of threads & meetings)
  • Automated meeting minutes and board packet generation
  • Pattern detection in maintenance and spending over time
  • Enhanced reporting and analytics for reserves & operations
  • Optional reserve investment coordination through approved partners

Outcome: A digital-first management model where the board stays in control, repetitive work is automated, and complex topics (projects, disputes, budgets) are easier to understand and explain.

Included in every HOAMX plan

Unified communications

Telegram captures letters, email, voicemail, SMS, and AI chat into one searchable timeline so boards can see the full story behind each decision.

Document-centered governance

Bylaws, budgets, insurance policies, and key notices live in the Document Vault, tied back to meetings, motions, and Telegram entries.

Board continuity

As board members rotate, HOAMX preserves the institutional memory: who decided what, when, and based on which documents or communications.

Professional services & project support

Annual professional services can be attached to Silver or Gold tiers, or engaged separately. Larger projects are scoped under a simple per-hour “Project Support” rate.

Accounting & Treasury

Budget preparation, reserve scheduling, bank reconciliation, and year-end reporting, aligned with Minnesota HOA financial expectations.

Legal & Compliance

Support for bylaws, notice rules, fines, and meet-and-confer procedures, plus contract templates and conflict-of-interest documentation in coordination with your chosen attorney.

Insurance & Vendor Oversight

Coordination around master policy renewals, vendor certificates of insurance, RFP cycles, and performance documentation for key vendors like landscape, snow, roofing, and maintenance.
