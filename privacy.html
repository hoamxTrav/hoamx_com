Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice explains how HOAMX collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and platform. We designed HOAMX to respect resident privacy while still giving boards and managers the documentation they need to operate responsibly.

Non-legal summary: This page explains our intentions and practices in clear language. It is not legal advice. Associations should review their own privacy and record policies with qualified counsel.

What this notice covers

Website visitors

People who browse hoamx.com or related demo sites and submit contact forms, demo requests, or other inquiries.

Boards & managers

Association representatives who use HOAMX to manage billing, maintenance, communications, governance, and documents.

Residents & vendors

Individuals whose information may be entered into HOAMX by their association (for example, owners, tenants, and service providers).

Information we collect

HOAMX is an operations and recordkeeping platform. Most information in the system is entered by board members, managers, or our own staff as they help run the association.

Contact and account details

Names, email addresses, phone numbers, mailing addresses, and association roles (for example, board member, manager, owner, tenant, vendor contact).

Association & property information

Association names, unit identifiers, billing status, maintenance history, policy documents, budgets, insurance details, and vendor contracts as provided by the board or manager.

Communications (“Telegram”)

Letters, emails, voicemail recordings and transcripts, text messages, and meeting notes that the board or manager chooses to store in HOAMX. These may include sender/recipient information and timestamps.

Usage and technical information

Log data related to how the website and platform are used (such as IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and time spent) used to maintain security and improve reliability.

Support interactions

Messages and call notes for support, training, or demo requests, including the content you share and our responses.

How we use information

We use information primarily to operate the HOAMX platform, support associations, and maintain reliable records. Typical examples include:

Operating the platform

Creating and managing association accounts, routing maintenance requests, logging board decisions, and generating reports for boards and managers.

Communications & recordkeeping

Delivering messages between boards, residents, vendors, and our support team, and storing those communications as part of the association’s historical record.

Security & reliability

Monitoring system performance, detecting misuse or suspicious activity, and applying technical and organizational measures to protect data.

Support & training

Responding to questions, troubleshooting issues, and providing training or guidance on how to use HOAMX features.

Product improvement

Aggregated or de-identified analysis of usage patterns to improve features and workflows. We aim to avoid using identifiable information for analytics whenever possible.

How we share information

HOAMX is not in the business of selling resident or association data. We share information only as needed to operate the platform and support your association.

Within the association

Boards, managers, and designated support staff can access the information they need to perform their roles. Access can be limited by role and permissions.

Service providers

We may use third-party providers for hosting, email delivery, call handling, or analytics. These providers are expected to use data only to provide their services to us.

Legal & safety

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or if we believe it is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of residents, associations, our team, or others.

We do not sell personal information to data brokers or advertisers. If that ever changes, we will update this notice and, where required, provide a straitforward way to opt out.

Cookies and analytics

Our website and platform may use cookies or similar technologies to keep you logged in and understand how features are used.

Essential cookies

Used for things like session management, security, and saving basic preferences. Without these, parts of the platform may not work correctly.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics tools to understand how people navigate the site so we can improve design and performance. We aim to avoid collecting more data than necessary.

Your choices

You can typically configure your browser to refuse or limit cookies. Doing so may affect certain features, such as staying signed in.

Data retention

HOAMX is built around long-lived records for governance and compliance. At the same time, we aim not to retain more information than necessary.

Association records

Minutes, motions, financial records, and key communications may be retained for many years, in line with typical HOA recordkeeping practices and the association’s own policies.

Platform logs

Technical logs used for security and diagnostics are generally retained for shorter periods and then deleted or anonymized.

Account closures

If an association stops using HOAMX, we will coordinate with the board on exporting core records and applying a reasonable retention or deletion schedule, subject to legal and contractual requirements.

Your rights & choices

Specific privacy rights depend on where you live and local law, but in general, you can ask us about the information we hold and request updates or corrections.

Residents & vendors

If your information appears in HOAMX because of an association you are part of, your first point of contact is usually your board or manager, who controls the association data.

Boards & managers

If you administer an HOAMX account, you can contact us directly to discuss configuration, access controls, and data requests from residents.

Contacting us

For privacy questions, you can email us at privacy@example-hoamx.com, and we’ll work with you and, where appropriate, your association’s leadership to respond.

Children and sensitive information

HOAMX is intended for use by adults who serve on boards, manage properties, or contract with associations.

Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Information about minors may appear in records only when provided by a parent, guardian, or association as part of normal operations.

Sensitive categories

HOAMX is not designed to store highly sensitive data such as detailed medical records, Social Security numbers, or payment card numbers. Boards and managers should avoid uploading such data and use appropriate specialized systems when needed.

Changes to this notice

We may update this Privacy Notice as our platform, practices, or applicable laws change. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, notify associations using HOAMX.
