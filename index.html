What HOAMX does for your community

Designed to support self-managed and professionally managed associations with the same tools: clear records, efficient operations, and simple communication.

How it works

1. Connect your board

We set up your HOA in HOAMX with roles for board members, managers, and vendors. No app install required—just email, web, and phone.

2. Centralize communication

Letters, emails, voicemails, and texts all feed into Telegram, your unified communication log. Every decision has a paper trail.

3. Run your operations

Use billing, work orders, and governance tools to manage the association day to day, with reports ready for audits and annual meetings.
